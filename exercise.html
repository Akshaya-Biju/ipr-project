Eye Exercises for Refreshing Your Vision

Here are some effective exercises you can do to keep your eyes healthy and reduce strain.

20-20-20 Rule

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds. This helps reset your focus and reduce strain.

Palming

Rub your palms together to generate heat, then gently cup your hands over your eyes. Relax for 30 seconds.

Blinking

Blink rapidly 10-15 times to refresh your eyes, improving moisture and circulation.

Focus Shifting

Focus on an object close to you, then quickly shift your focus to something farther away. Repeat for 30 seconds.

Eye Rolling

Slowly roll your eyes in a circle, first clockwise, then counterclockwise, to relax the eye muscles.

Near and Far Focus

Hold your thumb 10 inches from your face, focus on it for 10 seconds, then switch your focus to an object 10 feet away.
